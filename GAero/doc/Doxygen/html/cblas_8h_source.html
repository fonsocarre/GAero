1 /* cblas.h
2  *
3  * This header defines C bindings for the Basic Linear Algebra Subprograms,
4  * providing optimized basic operations on vectors and matrices. Single-
5  * and double-precision, real and complex data formats are supported by this
6  * library.
7  *
8  * A note on complex data layouts:
9  *
10  * In order to allow straightforward interoperation with other libraries and
11  * complex types in C and C++, complex data in BLAS is passed through an opaque
12  * pointer (void *). The layout requirements on this complex data are that
13  * the real and imaginary parts are stored consecutively in memory, and have
14  * the alignment of the corresponding real type (float or double). The BLAS
15  * complex interfaces are compatible with the following types:
16  *
17  * - The C complex types, defined in <complex.h>.
18  * - The C++ std::complex types, defined in <complex>.
19  * - The LAPACK complex types, defined in <Accelerate/vecLib/clapack.h>.
20  * - The vDSP types DSPComplex and DSPDoubleComplex, defined in <Accelerate/vecLib/vDSP.h>.
21  * - An array of size two of the corresponding real type.
22  * - A structure containing two elements, each of the corresponding real type.
23  *
24  */
